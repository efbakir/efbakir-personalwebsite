Finova Banking App

YEAR 2025
TYPE UX Design
ROLE Lead Product Designer

OVERVIEW

End-to-end UX research and design for a digital banking experience focused on financial literacy. Conducted user interviews, journey mapping, and iterative prototyping to create an accessible mobile-first product.

PROBLEM

Traditional banking apps focus on transactions but don't help users understand the "why" behind their financial behavior. Young users especially struggle with financial literacy.

RESEARCH

  • 73% of users aged 18-30 felt confused by banking terminology
  • Users wanted contextual explanations without leaving their workflow
  • Visual spending patterns were more effective than raw numbers
  • Gamification helped goal-setting but felt manipulative when overused

SOLUTION

We created an educational layer within the banking experience. Contextual tooltips, smart insights, and a learning center that adapts to each user's financial behavior.

KEY FEATURES

  • Smart Insights: AI-driven spending analysis with plain-language explanations
  • Learning Moments: Contextual financial education in the transaction flow
  • Goal Tracking: Visual progress tracking for savings goals
  • Spending Patterns: Visual breakdown with actionable recommendations

OUTCOME

Post-launch metrics showed a 45% increase in user engagement with financial planning features. Support tickets related to feature confusion decreased by 60%.
